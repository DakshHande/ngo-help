Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. At Our NGO, we are fully committed to safeguarding any personal information you share with us and ensuring transparency about how we use and protect that data. This Privacy Policy details what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to ensure your data remains secure and confidential.

Our pledge to you: We will never sell, trade, or misuse your personal information. Your trust is our priority, and we operate with full transparency.

No Account Creation Required

You are welcome to browse our website freely without needing to create an account or sign up. We do not require personal login credentials, usernames, or passwords to access the information or features available on our site. Your interaction with our website remains anonymous unless you voluntarily provide personal information through forms or donations.

Information We Collect

While we do not collect data for general browsing, we do collect certain personal information when you choose to engage with us directly (e.g., when making donations or contacting us). The types of personal data we may collect include:

  • Name: Provided voluntarily when you make a donation or reach out via our contact forms.
  • Email Address: Collected for donation receipts, updates, or responses to inquiries. We only use this information to communicate with you and do not share it with third parties.
  • Payment Information: For donations, we securely collect your payment details via QR code transactions. Rest assured, we never store your credit card details, and our payment methods are secure and encrypted.

How We Use Your Information

We collect personal information solely for the following purposes:

  • Processing Donations: We use your payment information to securely process donations and ensure that your contributions are directed to the intended cause. We prioritize the security of your financial data and use encrypted payment gateways to protect your details.
  • Communication and Updates: Your email may be used to send donation receipts, thank you messages, or updates on our work and projects. You can choose to opt out of receiving non-essential updates at any time.
  • Legal and Compliance Purposes: We may retain certain information for auditing or regulatory compliance, as required by law, including ensuring that we adhere to tax and donation-related legal obligations.

QR Code Payments for Enhanced Security

We prioritize your privacy by using a QR code-based payment system, which eliminates the need for third-party processors. This ensures that your financial details remain secure throughout the transaction. We do not store credit card information, and your data is handled exclusively by us through encrypted and secure channels.

Data Security Practices

Data security is one of our top priorities. We employ the following measures to ensure your personal data remains safe:

  • SSL Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology, ensuring that sensitive information is protected during transmission.
  • Restricted Access: Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only, and all staff members are trained on best practices for data protection.
  • Secure Storage: We use industry-standard encryption protocols to store any personal data, minimizing the risk of unauthorized access or breaches.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use any cookies or tracking technologies to monitor your browsing behavior on our site. Your visit remains anonymous unless you voluntarily share your information. We respect your privacy and ensure that your experience with us is free from intrusive data tracking.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade your personal information with third-party marketers or advertisers. Your information is only shared in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in processing donations or fulfilling legal obligations (e.g., auditors, regulators) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law to comply with legal proceedings, investigations, or regulatory requirements.

Your Rights Regarding Personal Data

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time. Should you wish to exercise any of these rights, please reach out to us at the email provided below, and we will respond promptly and in compliance with applicable laws.

Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy. Our services are not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove that information.

Policy Changes and Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as needed to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or improvements to our services. Any significant changes will be communicated through updates on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.
