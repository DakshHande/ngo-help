Privacy Policy

Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information that you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use and protect that information, and the steps we take to ensure transparency and security.

No Login or Sign-Up Required

Our website does not require users to create an account, sign up, or log in. You can browse and use all features of the site freely without needing to provide personal login credentials. We do not store usernames, passwords, or any other authentication data.

Information We Collect

Although we do not require any login, certain personal information may be collected in specific contexts, such as when you make a donation or contact us. The types of personal data we may collect include:

  • Name: When you donate, you may choose to provide your name for our records.
  • Email Address: We collect your email address when you donate or contact us to provide receipts, respond to inquiries, and share important updates.
  • Payment Information: We only collect payment information through secure QR code transactions to ensure maximum safety. No third-party websites or external payment apps are used to process your donations.

How We Use Your Information

We collect and use your personal data solely for the following purposes:

  • Processing Donations: We use your payment information to process donations through secure QR code transactions. We ensure that no third-party sites or applications are involved in the process to protect your financial details.
  • Communication: We may use your email address to send donation confirmations, updates on our activities, or respond to your inquiries. You can opt out of receiving updates at any time.
  • Legal Requirements: We may be required to retain certain information to comply with legal obligations or for auditing purposes.

QR Code Payment for Security

We only accept donations via QR code payment methods to provide a direct and secure method of transaction. By eliminating third-party websites or payment processors, we ensure that your financial data is safe and handled only by us. You can confidently make donations knowing that your personal and financial information will remain secure and private.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use any cookies, tracking pixels, or other technologies to track your activity or store information about your browsing habits. Your visit to our site remains anonymous unless you voluntarily provide information through donations or communications.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and employ reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Our website uses SSL encryption to ensure that all data transmitted between your browser and our servers is secure.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only shared with trusted partners for specific donation processing or as required by law (e.g., legal obligations, compliance with regulations).

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information that we store. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below. We will respond to your request in compliance with applicable laws.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take appropriate steps to remove such information from our records.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify users of any significant changes by updating the date of the policy at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
