Cost Transparency: How We Run Our Website

Our Commitment to Financial Transparency

We believe in complete transparency regarding the costs associated with running our website. This page outlines all expenses to show how we ensure that 100% of your donations are directed toward helping those in need, without using them for operational costs like web hosting or development.

Breakdown of Website Costs (in INR)

Domain Registration: ₹1,200/year
The domain is the web address you use to find our site. We renew this annually to keep our presence online.
Web Hosting: ₹10,000/year
Hosting is what allows our website to stay online 24/7. We use reliable and secure servers to ensure no downtime and full security for visitors.
Website Development and Maintenance: ₹25,000/year
Our dedicated team ensures that the website runs smoothly, is regularly updated, and remains secure from any technical vulnerabilities. This includes ongoing development, maintenance, and updates.
Security and Backup Services: ₹5,000/year
To protect our website and your personal data, we invest in security systems and regular backups to safeguard our content and prevent data breaches.
Content Management System (CMS): ₹4,000/year
The CMS allows us to manage and publish content on the website easily. This cost covers the use of software tools and resources.
Miscellaneous Expenses: ₹3,500/year
This includes additional resources such as plugins, design themes, and any minor adjustments that enhance the website’s performance and appearance.
Total Annual Website Cost: ₹48,700

How We Cover These Costs

These costs are not covered by donor contributions. Instead, they are funded through:

  • Google AdSense revenue from ads on certain pages
  • Sponsorship agreements and corporate partnerships
  • Special fundraising initiatives targeting operational costs

Ensuring Trust and Accountability

We want you to feel confident about where your contributions go. All operational costs are covered independently through ads, sponsorships, and other revenue sources. This allows us to direct 100% of your donations toward our programs in India, helping those in need directly.

We provide financial reports and regular updates on our website to keep you informed about how we manage costs and ensure transparency. If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out to us, and we will be happy to provide any further details.
